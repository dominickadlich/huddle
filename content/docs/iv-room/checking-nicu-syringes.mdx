## Always 
- Send ready to administer 
- Make sure correct dilution is used if applicable 
- Must use preservative free products if possible 

## Attach tubing 

Intermittent IV for NICU floor 
- Tubing must be attached, primed, capped & clamped 
- Label must be on the tubing but not covering where the syringe attaches to tubing  
- Recipe must be made to account for tubing overfill if drug was not pre-diluted (ex: Ampho B) 
- Label will indicate “NICU Tubing Required” 

## Do not attach tubing  

- All IV dose for patients on the PEDS/PICU floors/Nursery (Unit 56N) 
- Continuous drips: Examples: dobutamine, dopamine, midazolam, insulin and morphine 
- SUBQ/IM. Example: Enoxaparin SUBQ/IM GET 0.1ML OVER FILL IF DOSE VOLUME IS LESS THAN 0.5ML 
- Replacement Fluids:  Examples: Sodium Acetate 0.63% & heparin, NS & D5 with heparin
